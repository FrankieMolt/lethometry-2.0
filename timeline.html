TEMPORAL ANOMALY

130 years of events that shouldn't align but do.

Every entry below is independently sourced. What makes this a timeline isn't the events themselves — it's the pattern. Premonitions written 14 years before they came true. Programmatic conspiracies declassified decades later. If reality is a simulation, this is the changelog.

1893

Baron Trump's Marvelous Underground Journey

Ingersoll Lockwood publishes a novel about Wilhelm Heinrich Sebastian Von Trump who travels to Russia and meets a guide named "Don." 123 years before the 45th President.

1898

Futility: The Wreck of the Titan

Morgan Robertson publishes a novel about the "Titan," an "unsinkable" ship that hits an iceberg. 14 years before the Titanic. Same death toll. Same month.

1901

Tesla's Wardenclyffe Tower

Tesla begins construction of a tower to provide free wireless energy. J.P. Morgan withdraws funding when he realizes it cannot be metered. Demolished in 1917.

1939

FDR Moves Thanksgiving

Roosevelt moves Thanksgiving one week earlier to extend Christmas shopping. The nation splits. Congress fixes it in 1941. The "tradition" is 83 years old.

1943

The Philadelphia Experiment

USS Eldridge allegedly made invisible at Philadelphia Naval Shipyard. Claims of teleportation and time displacement. No Navy record. Origin: Carlos Allende's 1956 letter.

1947

De Beers: "A Diamond is Forever"

The marketing campaign that invented the diamond engagement ring "tradition." Before 1947: 10% had diamonds. By 1990: 80%.

1952

Hydrogen Bomb + UFO Spike

US detonates Ivy Mike. UFO sightings spike 162% in November 1952. The nuclear-UFO correlation has repeated throughout history.

1952

Project Blue Book Begins

US Air Force establishes UFO study program. 12,000 sightings investigated over 17 years. 701 remain unexplained. Program ends 1969.

1953

MKUltra Begins

CIA begins 20 years of mind control experiments. LSD, sensory deprivation, electroshock. Helms destroys most records in 1973.

1962

Operation Northwoods

Joint Chiefs propose staging terrorist attacks on US soil and blaming Cuba. JFK rejects it. Declassified in 2001.

1997

HAARP Activated

High Frequency Active Auroral Research Program goes live in Gakona, Alaska. 180 antennas, 3.6 megawatts. Conspiracy theories follow: earthquakes, weather control, mind control.

2003

Bostrom's Simulation Argument

Nick Bostrom publishes the trilemma: civilizations go extinct, lose interest in simulations, or we are almost certainly simulated.

2012

CERN Discovers Higgs Boson

LHC discovers the "God particle." Mandela Effect goes viral globally. The correlation is documented.

2023

UAP Government Admission

US government confirms UAPs are real. David Grusch testifies under oath about recovered "non-human" craft.
