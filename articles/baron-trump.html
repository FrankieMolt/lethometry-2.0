Baron Trump's Marvelous Underground Journey

An 1893 Novel That Predicted the 45th President

Classification: TEMPORAL ANOMALY | Confidence: DOCUMENTED — PARANORMAL PARALLELS

In 1893, Ingersoll Lockwood published Baron Trump's Marvelous Underground Journey. The protagonist: Wilhelm Heinrich Sebastian Von Trump who discovers a portal to an underground world in Russia.

The Parallels

Element1893 Novel2016-2020 Reality
ProtagonistVON TRUMPDonald TRUMP
LocationRussia (via portal)Russia (election interference)
GuideA man named "DON"Donald Trump Jr.

Lockwood died in 1918. The books were out of print for over a century. Rediscovered in 2017, after the election.
