SIMULATION CALCULATOR

Based on Nick Bostrom's trilemma and modern evidence. Answer honestly.

In 2003, philosopher Nick Bostrom published the simulation argument: civilizations either go extinct, lose interest in running simulations, or we are almost certainly living in one. These six questions are an informal test of how compatible your worldview is with that trilemma. There are no wrong answers. Only probabilities.

// PROBABILITY ANALYSIS //

Q1. Do you believe the universe's physical constants are fine-tuned?

Q2. Have you experienced a Mandela Effect you cannot explain?

Q3. Do you think governments have lied about major events?

Q4. Does quantum mechanics suggest reality is not what it seems?

Q5. Could advanced civilizations simulate consciousness?

Q6. Have you ever felt reality "glitch"?
